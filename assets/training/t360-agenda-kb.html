Introduction

Overview

The Agenda Creator is T360's flagship meeting preparation feature. It automatically generates your entire Toastmasters meeting agenda based on role bookings and assignments — drastically reducing the time ExComm members spend on agenda prep.

⏱️
Time Savings
What normally takes 30–45 minutes of manual effort can now be completed in under 5 minutes using the Auto Fill feature.

The Agenda Creator consists of two major sections:

⚙️
Agenda Settings
Controls visibility, public layout style, banner colors, and how the agenda is shared.
Settings
📝
Agenda Section
Where the actual meeting agenda is auto-generated and customized with full editorial control.
Builder
🔐
Permissions

Who Can Edit

⚠️
ExComm Members Only
Only ExComm (Executive Committee) members can access and edit the meeting agenda. Regular members can view the published agenda but cannot modify it.

Meeting › Open Meeting › Edit Agenda

Once inside the meeting view, click Edit Agenda to begin preparing the meeting agenda directly.

Agenda Settings · 1 of 3

Visibility Control

While preparing the agenda, you may not want members to see an incomplete version. T360 lets you toggle visibility on or off at any point during editing.

off
Visibility OFF — Draft Mode
Members cannot view the agenda. A message will indicate that the VP Education or ExComm is currently preparing the agenda, avoiding confusion during preparation.
on
Visibility ON — Published
Once the agenda is ready, toggle visibility ON to make it instantly available to all members and guests.

Agenda Builder · Core Feature

Auto Fill Entire Agenda

This is the most powerful feature of the Agenda Creator. With a single click, T360 checks all booked meeting roles and automatically fills every agenda section.

✨
How to Trigger
Click the Auto Fill Entire Agenda button in the Agenda Section. T360 will instantly generate the complete agenda based on all current role bookings and assignments.

The system auto-populates details for every booked role:

✓SAA (Sergeant at Arms) details
✓Presiding Officer information
✓Toastmaster of the Day (TMOD)
✓Theme of the Day
✓Speaker booking and speech info
✓Evaluator assignments linked

Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can edit the agenda? ▼
Only ExComm members can create or edit the agenda. Regular members and guests can only view the published version.
Can I hide the agenda while preparing it? ▼
Yes. Turn Agenda Visibility OFF while editing. Members will see a message indicating the agenda is being prepared, preventing confusion over incomplete drafts.
Do I need to manually enter every role? ▼
No. Use the Auto Fill Entire Agenda button and T360 will automatically populate all booked roles and meeting details based on current assignments.
Can I customize auto-filled sections? ▼
Yes. Every section can still be manually edited after auto-generation. Auto-fill is a starting point, not a lock-in.
Can I remove sections from the agenda? ▼
Yes. Use the Eye icon on any section to hide it from the published agenda. You can re-enable hidden sections at any time.
Can I rearrange the agenda order? ▼
Yes. Use the Up/Down arrow controls on each section to reorder the meeting flow in any sequence that fits your club's format.
Can members access evaluation forms through the agenda? ▼
Yes. Prepared Speaker sections automatically include embedded evaluation forms. Anyone opening the agenda link can access the form directly without any separate sharing.
Can I share the agenda on WhatsApp? ▼
Yes. Simply copy the agenda link and share it via WhatsApp, email, social media, or any other channel. The same link works everywhere.
What happens if I update the agenda after sharing? ▼
No problem at all. The agenda is live. The same shared link automatically reflects the latest updates without any action required from you.
Do I need to resend the agenda link after making changes? ▼
No. The shared link always shows the latest version of the agenda. You never need to create a new link or resend anything after making updates.
