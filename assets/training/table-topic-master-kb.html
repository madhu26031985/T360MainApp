How to Book the Table Topic Master Role

Before you can manage Table Topics, you must first book the Table Topic Master role. You can do this in two ways:

1

From Book a Role

Meeting → Open Meeting → Book a Role
  1. Open the meeting
  2. Tap Book a Role
  3. Go to the Open section
  4. Find Table Topic Master
  5. Tap Book
2

From the Home Page

A dedicated tab on the Home page for faster access.

  1. Open the Home page
  2. Tap the Table Topics tab
  3. If the role is available, tap Book
⚡ A faster way to reserve the role without navigating to the full role booking page.

The Table Topic Corner Page

After booking, the Table Topic Corner page becomes your dedicated workspace. It contains two tabs that work together to help you prepare and preserve your session:

Tab 1

Table Topic Corner

Your private workspace to prepare questions before and during the meeting.

  • Add Table Topic questions in advance
  • Prepare impromptu speaking prompts
  • Edit or refine questions anytime
  • Build your session digitally
Tab 2

Table Topic Summary

The member-facing view — published after the session concludes.

  • Members can see all questions asked
  • Speakers can revisit prompts anytime
  • Supports self-improvement at home
  • Builds a growing knowledge base
💡 Tap + Add Question inside Table Topic Corner to continuously build your complete set of questions before the meeting starts.

Controlling Visibility — The Eye Button

At the top of the Table Topic Corner page, you will see Show Table Topic Summary to Member along with an Eye button. This controls whether members can view your prepared questions.

🔒 Eye OFF

Hidden from Members

Keep the Eye button OFF before and during the meeting to preserve the spontaneity of the session.

  • Questions remain private
  • Members cannot see prompts
  • Speakers won't know questions in advance
  • You can freely add or modify questions
👁 Eye ON

Visible to Members

Turn the Eye button ON after the Table Topics session is completed to publish all questions asked.

  • Questions visible in Table Topic Summary
  • Members can revisit anytime
  • Speakers can reflect and practise again
  • Club users can learn from past questions

Club-wide Table Topic Question Repository

Every published Table Topic question is automatically saved and made available club-wide. Over time, this builds a central pool of impromptu speaking prompts for your club.

📚 Questions saved under the Club section

How the Role is Performed in T360

The Table Topic Master role in T360 is fully digital. Here is the complete end-to-end flow:

1
Book the role Reserve the Table Topic Master role from the Home page or via Book a Role.
2
Open the Table Topic Corner page Access your dedicated workspace immediately after booking.
3
Add your questions Use + Add Question to prepare all Table Topic prompts in advance.
4
Keep questions private Leave the Eye button OFF before and during the meeting to maintain spontaneity.
5
Conduct the Table Topics session Ask the prepared impromptu questions to speakers during the meeting.
6
Publish the questions Turn ON Show Table Topic Summary to Member after the session ends.
7
Members review later Questions become available in Table Topic Summary and are saved under the Club section for future reference.
